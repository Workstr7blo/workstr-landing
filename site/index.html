What Workstr is

A workout tracker designed for ownership.

Workstr starts as a practical training workspace: create exercises, organize programs, run sessions, and track progress. The app stores data in your browser first, so the product can work without a traditional backend account.

Train

Plan and log real sessions.

Build structured workouts around sets, reps, rest, equipment, muscles, and coaching notes instead of loose text notes.

Own

Your data starts with you.

Workstr is local-first. Your browser database is the source of truth before any publishing or syncing layer is added.

Publish

Nostr-native by design.

Public exercises, programs, and training content can become signed Nostr events, making useful routines portable across relays and clients.

Key safety

Sign in with a signer. Never paste your private key.

Workstr delegates signing to a Nostr signer you control. The app should never ask for, store, or display your nsec.
